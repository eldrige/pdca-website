About PDCA

PCDA.....Proud of our unique identity and out for the betterment of the community, solely declare that we constitute one and the same believe, bound by the same destiny and and asset our firm determination to build the Cameroon community on the basis of the ideals of humanitarians and care,peace,justice and progress

Resolved to establish a national community based Non' Governmental organization in order to ensure the wellbeing of every Cameroonian indigent without discrimination, to devote all our efforts to that end.Declare our readiness to cooperate with all stakeholders that desire to participate in this community endeavor.

The constitution has however been structured on January 20th 2020. This association does not operate under the umbrella of any political party but has an observation status in the case of elections of any type
